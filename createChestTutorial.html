How to create a chest in RPG Maker!


In this tutorial we will be going over how to create a chest in RPG Maker along with what are some of the posibilities that can be used when creating chests


When you load up the map the first thing you want to do is click the little red guy in the middle top left of the screen. This is the event creator. The event creator is exactly what it sounds like... it creates events. Now events basicaly are things that happen in the game. A really good game will have tons of these and really are the heart and soul of the game.
